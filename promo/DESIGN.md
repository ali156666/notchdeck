# 悬屿 Promo Design

## Style Prompt
Apple-style product film on a white stage. Soft white/gray background, huge confident black typography, sparse copy, and the real app UI floating like hardware. The interface is revealed in slow pushes and precise fades, with small colored status dots as the only accents. Motion should feel like a keynote product reveal: quiet, premium, clean.

## Colors
- Background: #f5f5f7
- Panel: #ffffff
- Panel Border: #d2d2d7
- Foreground: #1d1d1f
- Muted Foreground: #6e6e73
- Accent Green: #29c869
- Accent Amber: #f5a524
- Accent Blue: #3478f6

## Typography
- Primary: SF Pro Display, PingFang SC, system-ui, sans-serif
- Mono: SF Mono, ui-monospace, monospace

## Motion
- Use GSAP/Remotion timeline choreography, not CSS animation.
- Animate transforms and opacity only.
- Product screenshots drift slowly while copy stays steady.
- Use soft crossfades, light sweeps, and 3-5 second product holds.

## What NOT to Do
- Do not use generic neon gradients or decorative blobs.
- Do not put the product screenshot inside another card-heavy mockup.
- Do not expose real clipboard/history/API content in marketing frames.
- Do not over-explain features; Apple-style copy stays short.
